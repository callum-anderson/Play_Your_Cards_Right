When the game starts, 5 cards are dealt and placed on the rack. Click on the first one to reveal it, and then click on Higher or Lower before clicking on the next card. If you guessed correctly (or the cards match), you get a point. If you guess incorrectly, the game ends.

If you reach the end of the rack, another 5 cards are dealt and placed on the rack, and the process repeats. You can attempt to change the current card up to 3 times by clicking on Change Card, but you must answer a quiz question before the card changes.

The game ends when there aren't enough cards left in the deck to play another round. In other words, there are less then 5 cards left in the deck. The maximum possible score is 40.
